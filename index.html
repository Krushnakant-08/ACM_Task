About ACM PCCoE

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is the world’s largest computing society. The ACM PCCoE Student Chapter fosters innovation, peer learning, and community engagement through technical and non-technical events.

Anantya is our annual celebration of technology, creativity, and collaboration — bringing together students from across disciplines to compete, learn, and grow.
